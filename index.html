Class Types

Hot Yoga Classes – 60 and 90 Minutes

The Bikram series is an intelligently designed sequence of 26 postures (asanas), practiced for 60 or 90 minutes in a hot room. The increased room temperature aids in warming the muscles and increasing the flexibility of the body tissues, allowing for deeper stretching and helping to prevent injury. Toxins are released through the breath and skin by sweating. The end result is an unparalleled feeling of mental and physical well-being and relaxation.

Inferno Hot Pilates

The HOTTEST new workout from New York and Las Vegas is now in Vancouver! Inferno Hot Pilates (Hot HIIT) is a fun, challenging, full body, low impact workout using Pilates principles and High Intensity Interval Training (HIIT). It combines cardio and muscle toning in a heated room and is performed on a yoga mat and towel. The High Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) keeps your heart rate up, helping burn fat and calories, and increase fitness levels without the pounding of a high impact workout. Designed for all fitness levels.

Warm Hatha Class

The warm hatha yoga class is designed for those who are new to yoga. This 60 minute class consists of 26 postures (1 set each) practiced at a slower pace and in warm room to provide a safe environment to stretch, strengthen and tone muscles.

Posture Clinics

Special clinics developed to help you deepen your practice and give you the one-on-one attention that isn’t always possible in a regular Hot Yoga 101 class.
